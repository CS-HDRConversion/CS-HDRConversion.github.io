CS HDR Conversion Program 2024

Providing Research Pathways to Honours and Master by Coursework Students in Computer Science

About

The HDR conversion program presents a selection of research topics for PhD and Master by Research programs offered by the UTS School of Computer Science. These projects are intended to provide Honours and Master by Coursework students with potential research pathways towards pursuing higher degrees by research (HDR).

More information about the HDR conversion program will be provided in the Information Session.

Contact

If you are interested in any of the projects listed above, please reach out to the supervisor of the project. The supervisor will be able to guide you through the PhD or Master by Research application processes.

If you would like to consider PhD (or Master by Research) enrolment in 2025, please be aware of the application deadlines.

If you are currently in the final year of your undergraduate studies and you'd like to pursue a PhD degree, we encourage you to consider completing a Master by Research project (or an Honours project) with the same supervisor from your desired PhD project. This will allow you to build a strong foundation for your research journey and facilitate a smoother transition to a higher research degree. But if you would like to pursue a Master by Research degree, you don't need to complete an Honours and you can apply it (with your supervisor's guidance) directly. More information will be provided in the info session.

Should you have any inquiries pertaining to the administrative aspects of this program or require clarification on general HDR-related matters, you can direct your questions to the program organisers at CS_HDRConversion@uts.edu.au.
